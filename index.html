Varad Dhat

👋 Hi! I am Varad Dhat, a recent graduate from the University of Washington, Seattle, with a Master’s Degree. I specialize in robotics, focusing on human-robot interaction in mobile robotics, with applications in home robotics, field robotics, and ground autonomy.

I currently work as a Research Assistant at the Human-Centered Robotics Lab at UW-CSE, where I develop task-based shared autonomy systems to enable seamless and safe human-robot collaboration in dynamic environments.
